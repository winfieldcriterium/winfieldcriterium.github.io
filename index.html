Rules

DECENCY: Please respect residents and families when changing for your event. Riders breaking indecency laws (i.e. nudity) will be disqualified, ticketed and possibly arrested.

PARKING: Park in designated areas only. The eastern shoulder of Winfield Road is open for parking. Parking in the lot for the basketball courts will get your car ticketed and towed. Parking in the neighborhood outside of the course is allowed, as long as you follow all parking restrictions on signs. If a resident tells you to move, please move, as we don't want to upset the residents.

  • No refunds
  • Promoter or officials can make course changes without prior notification.
  • Races can be shortened by the promoter or officials.

Win A Bike

Prairie Path Cycles is raffling off 2 Trek bicycles! The kids bike is a Trek MT 200 valued at $309.99 and the adult bike is a Trek 7.1 FX valued at $439.99.


Trek MT 200 - Similar to the kid's raffle prize


Trek 7.1 FX - Similar to the adult raffle prize

How to win

Complete the Illinois Bicycle Safety Quiz Challenge. Entrants will earn 1 raffle ticket for each level completed. Adults can earn up to 6 raffle tickets by completing all 3 levels of the ADULT BICYCLIST and MOTORIST quizzes. Kids can earn up to 3 raffle tickets by completing all 3 levels under the CHILD BICYCLIST quiz.

The following is what each certificate is valued at in raffle tickets:

  • 1 Ticket - Bronze Level Certificate
  • 2 Tickets - Silver Level Certificate
  • 3 Tickets - Gold Level Certificate

Kids Course Code Information

Quiz Code Number: 385806001

Print out certificate

If the Bronze or Silver levels are completed, go back into the highest level completed and click on the Exit Test link at the bottom right of the page. The entrant will then see a button at the top that will allow them to print out the certificate.

If all 3 levels are complete, at the end of the quiz, the entrant will see a button that will allow them to print out the certificate.

Entrants may present their certificate at the Winfield Bikeways tent or email certificates to raffle@winfieldcriterium.com in order to redeem their raffle tickets. Raffle will take place Friday, July 17, 2015 at 8PM. Winner will be notified by email and posted on this website.
